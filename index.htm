Who I Am

I have a passion for all things data! I strive to consume and analyze everything around me in the pursuit of driving swift and accurate decisions and uncovering the unknown. Only once you have all the data can you truly understand what it was you were looking for. I love to diversify my knowledge by understanding as many fields of study as possible. This is critical to fully understanding the world around us.

I currently function as a front-end developer – designing and wrapping skins around Java applications hosted within our content management system, Liferay. I leverage HTML, CSS, JavaScript/jQuery along with frameworks such as Flexbox and Bootstrap and tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and Sketch to materialize great projects. What makes me great isn't the languages I know or the tools I use, but my attention to details that matter. My goal, as always, is to develop an elegant solution with which to provide the user a coherent, information-rich experience – ensuring a beautifully-designed and pleasant-to-navigate application which serves to bolster conversion rates and retain the current user base. I am passionate about providing great user experiences and seek inspiration from every source around me and strive to try to implement something new every day.

Previously I worked in the power industry providing predictive analytics for critical-to-generation machinery through statistical analysis and modeling. My typical day involved raw data manipulation through the help of Ruby, VBA, and R combined with results from advanced pattern recognition / machine learning algorithms to identify machinery failures with advanced notice.
